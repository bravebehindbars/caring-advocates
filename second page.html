    A natural human response to shared adversity.

    Most people who"ve been through hard times empathize with and have an urge to reach out to others who struggle with problems that feel similar to their own. For example, an older woman with children shares her experiences with an over-whelmed new mother. A widow offers tea and words of comfort to a woman whose husband has recently died. The desire for Advocate relationships can be seen as a natural human response to shared struggles.
